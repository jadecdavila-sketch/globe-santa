Tell us your story.

Each year, Boston families write to Globe Santa about their children at the holidays. We read every letter. If you live in our delivery area and have children 12 or under, we'll send a gift box that arrives before Christmas.

On Christmas Day, above all other days, a child should be like all others his age; he should not be forgotten. The Credo of Globe Santa, 1968

Before we begin

We'll ask for your name, address, your children's information, and a letter telling us about your family. Your information is private and used only to send your gifts. The Boston Globe Foundation may share excerpts from your letter (without your name) in fundraising stories — you can opt out on the next-to-last step.
